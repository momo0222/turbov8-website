Who We Are

Our Goal

We are a community based team filled with youth STEM ambassadors. We hope to inspire and recruit passionate individuals and provide a powerful learning experience through robotics. By learning from each other and seeking daily growth, we transcend traditional disciplinary boundaries in our drive towards the FIRST World Championship.

What We Do

We often passionately interact with community members both locally and internationally, collaborating and mentoring others in STEM and robotics. TurboV8 has frequent team meetings to communicate and build our robots for every season.
