2022-2023

TurbΩ V8 has had an exciting 2022-2023 FIRST Tech Challenge season. Throughout this year, we’ve made great strides in all aspects of a FIRST team, working to accomplish our 10 goals for the season.

Design Overview

This season, we went through many complete rebuilds and iterations. We had a total of more than 17 small rebuilds, but it can be classified in 4 main rebuilds. By the end, we had a super-improved high speed pitch system, a turret rotation speed up to 200° per second, total field control, and a latch for our deposit to ensure accuracy.
