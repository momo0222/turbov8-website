Robot Specifications

  • Dimensions: 24x18x12 inches
  • Weight: 30 pounds
  • Drive System: Belted Custome Drivetrain
  • Arm Extension: 28 inches

Hardware & Software

Our robot was built using a combination of custom-designed hardware and the following software:

  • Custom drivetrain
  • Scissor linkage - shock absorbent
  • Counter springing system
  • Turret and pitch with worm gear
  • Custom deposit basket
  • 3 Odometry pods
  • Pose exponential algorithm
  • PID controller
  • OpenCv webcam
  • Computer vision
